Wisconsin

Wisconsin is home to 12 federally-recognized tribes. It is home to tribes who
have both been here forever, and were forced to relocate here.

Tribes include Ojibwe, Potawatomi, Mohegan, Pequot, Niantic, Narragansett,
Montaukett, Tunxis, Munsee, Ho-chunk, Menominee, and Oneida.
