Siouan

The Siouan Language family is made up of languages from nations alongside the Mississippi river area. Nations within this family make up the Seven Council Fires, having similar cultures and all speaking different dialects of the same language. The Seven Council Fires is formally referred to as the Oceti Sakowin Oyate, and speak Dakota, Lakota, and Nakota. Within the area of the Siouan language family, there are many nations who speak other similar languages as well.
